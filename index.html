How it works

Three steps. One click from your side.

Everything after step two is automatic. See the full walkthrough →

1
Patients join your waitlist
Share your unique /join/your-clinic link. Patients fill in name, mobile, and appointment type. No app download required.
One-time setup
2
A slot opens
Mark a slot as cancelled or no-show in SlotPilot. That’s the one action your staff ever takes.
15 seconds of staff time
3
SlotPilot handles the rest
System texts the next matching patient, waits for YES/NO, books the slot, confirms, and schedules reminders. All automatic.
Zero staff involvement
