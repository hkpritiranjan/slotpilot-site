How it works

From cancelled slot to filled appointment — in 15 minutes.

SlotPilot automates the entire process of recovering a lost clinic slot. Here’s exactly what happens, step by step, from the clinic’s perspective and the patient’s.

1
Setup — one time
Register your clinic and share the join link
Takes 10 minutes. Create a free account, enter your clinic name, configure your appointment types (e.g. Initial Assessment, Follow-up, Dry Needling), and you’re given a public link your patients can use to join your waitlist.
What you set up once
Clinic name, address, practitioner names
Appointment types (each type has its own waitlist queue)
Slot duration (15, 30, 45, or 60 minutes)
Your patient join link: slotpilot.app/join/your-clinic-name
How patients join your waitlist
Share the link anywhere: WhatsApp, your email footer, a printed card at reception, your Practo profile bio, Instagram bio. Patients open it on their phone, enter name + mobile number + which appointment type they need, and they’re in. No app, no account, 90 seconds.
2
Daily workflow
A patient cancels (or doesn’t show up)
When a booked patient cancels by call or WhatsApp, your receptionist opens SlotPilot and clicks Cancel on that slot. If a patient simply didn’t show up, they click No-show. That’s the only action your staff takes.

3
Automatic
SlotPilot texts the next patient on the waitlist
The system scans the waitlist for the next patient who needs an Initial Assessment (matching the cancelled slot’s type), ordered by priority then join date. It sends them an SMS instantly.

4
15-minute window
Patient replies YES — slot is booked instantly
If the patient replies YES within 15 minutes, the slot is booked under their name, a confirmation SMS is sent, and the slot card in your dashboard updates immediately. Your receptionist sees this happen — they don’t do anything.
If the patient replies NO, or doesn’t respond in 15 minutes, the cascade moves on automatically to the next person on the list.

5
Automatic reminders
Reminders go out automatically — no action needed
Every booked patient (including waitlist fills and original bookings) automatically receives reminder SMS messages. This reduces no-show rates by 30–40%.

Clinic workflows

Works the same whether you use Practo or not

SlotPilot doesn’t replace your booking system. It plugs into the gap: the slot that just opened that your booking system can’t fill automatically.

📋 Clinic using Practo
New patients book via Practo as normal. Your existing patients who’ve asked to be notified of openings join your SlotPilot waitlist. When a Practo booking cancels, you mark it in SlotPilot. It fills the gap from your local waitlist — Practo never knows, and you don’t have to chase anyone.
📋 Clinic without Practo
Most of your patients come by word-of-mouth or walk-in. You add their upcoming bookings to SlotPilot manually. Patients who call asking about openings are told to join the waitlist via the link. When anything cancels, SlotPilot handles the recovery — you don’t call anyone.
⇧ Priority queue use case
A post-surgery patient needs physiotherapy within the week but your calendar is full. Add them to the waitlist and mark them as high priority. The next time any Initial Assessment cancels, they’re the first person texted — before any standard waitlist patient.
💬 WhatsApp (coming soon)
SMS delivery in India can be unreliable. The WhatsApp Business API integration will replace SMS entirely — same YES/NO reply flow, but through WhatsApp where Indian patients actually respond. Expected in the next release.
